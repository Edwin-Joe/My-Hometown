Padmanabhapuram is the former capital city of the erstwhile Hindu kingdom of Travancore.

The palace is complex inside with an old granite fortress around four kilometers long.

The palace is located at the foot of the Veli Hills, which forms a part of the Western Ghats.

The river Valli flows nearby.Another palace known as Kuttalam Palace is situated in Kuttalam in Tenkasi district, Tamil Nadu which is also under the ownership of Kerala government.
